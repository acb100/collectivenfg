NYT NAVN

NYT NAVN is a personal project from the electronic artist behind Shrooma, known for psytrance. While Shrooma dives into high-energy, hypnotic beats, NYT NAVN explores trance and electronica in a more experimental, immersive way.

Tracks often start with a simple idea – a loop, a beat, a mood – and grow into danceable, immersive pieces. Each track is designed to be felt as much as it is heard, whether alone with headphones or together on the dancefloor.

NYT NAVN thrives on experimentation, letting sound lead the way and embracing unexpected textures and twists. Covers, visuals, and other artistic elements are evolving, but the music itself remains the centerpiece: expressive, dynamic, and ready to move its audience.
