Shrooma

Shrooma is the psytrance project of the artist behind NYT NAVN. Shrooma explores high-energy, hypnotic beats, dark grooves, and mind-bending soundscapes designed for the dancefloor and festival stages.

The project emphasizes driving rhythms, intricate textures, and immersive atmospheres. Shrooma's tracks are built to take listeners on a journey, blending powerful basslines with evolving melodies and psychedelic layers.

While NYT NAVN focuses on experimental trance and electronica, Shrooma dives deep into the psychedelic side of electronic music, giving fans hypnotic, energetic experiences in both live and studio contexts.
